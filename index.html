Methodology

How We Score

Each service is evaluated across 10 categories on a 0-10 scale (10 = most predatory). Categories: data selling, content ownership, forced arbitration, terms changeability, deletion difficulty, data retention, location tracking, cross-platform sharing, AI training, and liability limitations.

The overall score is calculated as 100 minus the average category score times 10. Grades: A (85-100), B (70-84), C (50-69), D (30-49), F (below 30).

Why This Matters Now

Within 12-18 months, most people will have AI agents acting on their behalf. Those agents will agree to terms of service 50 times a day — booking, buying, signing up, negotiating.

A human might agree to 5 ToS per week. Your agent will do 50 per day. Without a sovereignty layer between your agent and the world, the privacy problem becomes catastrophic.
