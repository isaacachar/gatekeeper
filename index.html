Methodology

How We Score

Each service is evaluated across 10 categories on a 0–10 scale (10 = most predatory). Categories: data selling, content ownership, forced arbitration, terms changeability, deletion difficulty, data retention, location tracking, cross-platform sharing, AI training, and liability limitations.

The overall score is calculated as 100 minus the average category score times 10.
